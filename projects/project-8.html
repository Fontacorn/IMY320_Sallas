BOOK CATALOUGE
2022
Ayaaz Street

A client asked for redesign for their mobile layout that will display a catalogue of books for their mobile bookstore website. They hate displaying things as cells and want traditional lists. The list should be informative and contain relevant information. For this project we applied our knowledge of Fritt’s Law to focus mobile devices useability to ensure that users are able to hit the correct targets.
