SHOE SITE LOGIN/SIGNUP
2022
Ayaaz Street

A client asked for us to create a new form for their on-site web system to document new stock that comes to the warehouse. Their information is quite extensive, and they would like the process of logging it in to be improved with some ‘User Experience’ design. The company uses the Google merchant product data specification as the guideline of what information we need on the system. For this project we applied our knowledge of Peak-End Rule to create highs for users to feel like the process is fast and easy and to look forward to the end.
